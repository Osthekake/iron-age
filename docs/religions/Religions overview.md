## [[Northherd Shamanism]]
Blood sacrifices (usually herd animals).
Dark journeys, where the shaman goes without any light for days.
Pray for protection, and growing herds, good hunting, and so on.

## [[Ironburner Ancestor Worship]]
Some ironburners worship the barrow men they descend from. This religion is practiced in secret, far from the cities.
They "visit" the dead ancestors by going through special stone archways. 
Ask ancestors for advice, and work together to keep back the darkness.

## [[Seringor - The Lord of Fire]]
The god of elves and westmen. Keeper of knowledge and driver of progress. Some dwarves also worship this god.
