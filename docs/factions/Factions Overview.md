# Historic cultures
## [[Ancient dragons and titans]]
Large skeleton fossils, scars in the landscape.

## [[Aryn Civilization]]
Stone ruins with lost technology across the western/central part of [[Merona]]. Coins and iron artifacts. (Ancient Elves)

## [[The Barrow Men]]
Barrow mounds, standing stones, bronze artifacts. (The first people to settle in the North, driven north by the Aryn.)

## [[Wagoners]]
Straight roads carved into the landscape in the south/eastern part of [[Merona]]. (Ancient Gnomes)

## [[Rhuul]]
Iron weapons. Small region in central [[Merona]] that had the forges for the Aryn (Ancient Dwarves) Destroyed by ancient tidal wave/flood.

## [[Aegon]]
Scattered stone forts in the southern desert. Water-themed undead.

# Current cultures

## [[Cerwin elves]]
Aryn Holdouts - Cities around central and southern continent. Stone buildings and wooden mechanics. Import wood and iron.
## [[Ironburners]]
Camps and small towns around the Inland Isles and the [[Ironcrest Range]]. (Player characters)
## [[Northherders]] 
Nomadic fishermen/animal herders in the [[Ironcrest Range]]. Shamanistic blood religion.

## [[Paer]]
Wood-dwelling halflings in the central deep woods.

## [[Westmen]] 
Monastic order (elf, halfling and human) with scattered strongholds along old Aryn sea routes. Religion of the sea.

## [[Hiren]] 
Dwarves and gnomes with strong stone defense structures in the central mountains

## [[Mar]] 
Giants and Hobgoblins that invaded from the east. Pirate and Bandit groups hide in old Aryn and Rhuul forts, and attack travelers and some hamlets
