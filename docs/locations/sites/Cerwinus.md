## Region
[[Elvenshade]]

## Population
- **[[Cerwin elves]]**: 60% - Noble families, merchants, and professional soldiers
- **Human Servants**: 25% - Craftsmen, laborers, and household staff
- **Halfling Assistants**: 10% - Gardeners, cooks, and specialized artisans
- **Other Races**: 5% - Traders, diplomats, and temporary visitors

## Architecture
Built from distinctive white marble quarried from nearby hills, with redwood timber imported from Whisperwood. Buildings feature elegant arches, tall spires, and intricate wooden mechanisms. Streets are laid out in organized patterns with raised wooden walkways to protect from damp conditions.

## Description
The largest [[Cerwin elves]] city and former capital of the Aryn Civilization. Built on a series of hills overlooking fertile valleys, the city serves as the primary center for Elven culture, trade, and military power in the region. The white stone architecture gleams in sunlight, while redwood mechanisms provide everything from automated doors to complex irrigation systems. The city maintains strict social hierarchy and traditional elven customs despite the changing world around them.
