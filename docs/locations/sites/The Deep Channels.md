## Region
[Mosswater](../regions/Mosswater.md)

## Population
- **Mosswater Fishers**: 80% - Fishing crews and boat operators
- **[Westmen](../../factions/Westmen.md) Researchers**: 10% - Scholars studying aquatic phenomena
- **[Cerwin elves](../../factions/Cerwin%20elves.md) Explorers**: 5% - Those investigating mysterious depths
- **Water Spirits**: 5% - Supernatural beings occasionally encountered

## Architecture
Specialized fishing platforms and docks built over deeper waters, using advanced wooden engineering to withstand mysterious currents. Features include observation platforms, storage facilities, and specialized boats for deep water navigation.

## Description
Areas of the lake where strange creatures are said to dwell, reflecting the magical nature of darkness and the mysterious depths of Mosswater. These deep channels contain aquatic phenomena that defy normal understanding, with local fishers reporting unusual encounters and strange behavior from fish stocks. The Deep Channels serve as both a resource for specialized fishing and a source of supernatural events that interest [Westmen](../../factions/Westmen.md) scholars and concern local communities. The area represents the boundary between known aquatic life and the magical mysteries of the deep.
