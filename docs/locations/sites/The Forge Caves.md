## Region
[Spine Peaks](../regions/Spine Peaks.md)

## Population
- **[Hiren](../../factions/Hiren.md) Smiths**: 70% - Master craftsmen and steel makers
- **[Hiren](../../factions/Hiren.md) Guardians**: 20% - Protectors of forge secrets
- **Apprentices**: 10% - Young smiths learning the craft

## Architecture
Natural cave complexes with built-up forge areas, ventilation systems, and security measures. Features include specialized forges, quenching pools, and storage areas for precious metals.

## Description
Where [Hiren](../../factions/Hiren.md) smiths create their renowned steel products, using techniques kept secret from outsiders. These natural cave complexes provide ideal conditions for metalworking, with natural ventilation, secure storage, and protection from the elements. The Forge Caves contain generations of accumulated knowledge about steel production, with specialized tools and processes that create weapons and tools superior to those made elsewhere. The caves are heavily guarded and their location is one of the most closely kept secrets in the Spine Peaks region.
