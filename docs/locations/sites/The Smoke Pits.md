## Population
- **[Ironburners](../../factions/Ironburners.md)**: 85% - Forge masters and charcoal specialists
- **[Hiren](../../factions/Hiren.md) Technicians**: 10% - Engineers and maintenance experts
- **Local Workers**: 5% - Support staff and laborers

## Architecture
Industrial complex built around numerous burning pits and forge facilities, with specialized ventilation and smoke management systems. Features include covered pits, storage buildings, and processing workshops.

## Description
Area where [Ironburners](../../factions/Ironburners.md) maintain their forge operations, constantly shrouded in smoke from numerous charcoal pits. This industrial complex represents the heart of Ironfen's metalworking industry, with constant activity fueling both local needs and broader trade. The Smoke Pits feature sophisticated ventilation systems designed to handle massive fuel production while protecting workers from hazardous fumes. This area is perpetually shrouded in smoke, creating a distinctive landmark that can be detected from miles away across the wetlands.
